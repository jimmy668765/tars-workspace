## ‚ö†Ô∏è Accounts to AVOID (Red Flags)

Based on the research criteria, these patterns indicate low-quality accounts:

1. **Pure News Aggregators** - Accounts that only repost headlines without analysis
2. **Shill Accounts** - Promote specific coins without disclosed positions or reasoning
3. **Signal Sellers** - Promise "guaranteed returns" or sell "secret signals"
4. **Hype-Only Voices** - Only post during pumps, silent during dumps
5. **No Verifiable Track Record** - Claims expertise without demonstrable history
6. **Constant Engagement Bait** - "Like if you agree," "Follow for follow" tactics
